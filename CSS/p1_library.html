Some Rules of Library:-

  1. Belongings: Items such as helmets, umbrellas, raincoats, and bags are prohibited inside the library. Users should store these at the designated checkpoint or library lobby area.

  2. Food and Drinks: Consumption of food and drinks (except plain water) is strictly prohibited within the library premises, including the 24x7 reading room.

  3. Silence and Conduct: Maintaining silence is mandatory. Group discussions are not allowed in the reading and stack areas to prevent disturbances.

  4. Use of Electronic Devices: Laptops, mobile phones, and tablets must be set to silent mode. Activities such as online gaming, chatting, and non-academic video streaming are strictly forbidden.

  5. Reservation of Seats: Reserving seats is not permitted. If a seat remains vacant for more than 30 minutes, other users are entitled to occupy it.

  6. Handling Library Materials: Users should treat all library materials, including books and journals, with care. Damaged items must be reported immediately, and any removal of materials without proper checkout is prohibited.

  7. Disciplinary Actions: Violations of library regulations may result in actions such as being asked to leave the library, suspension of borrowing privileges, or referral to the Disciplinary Action Committee (DAC).
